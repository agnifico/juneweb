Arboreum [WIP]

Arboreum is a gamified notepad app. Every time you create a note, a tree is placed on a map. As you write more, watch your garden grow!
This app is inspired by the UI of old Pokemon Games, and the UX of an app like Finch or Habitica. This is something similar; collect all the trees in your Arboreum.
NOTE: The app is slow rn, mainly due to a "free tier" server xD
