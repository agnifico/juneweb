Water : Interwoven Fates

Although I'd spent most of my life either studying to be, or being a software developer, 7 years out of college in tech was as much as I could take. I quit my life of corporate computer science behind in early 2024, and after a short spell of aimlessness, the answer was clear as ever. I have always wanted to, and now will be an artist. More specifically, a designer. What do I want to design? Graphics, products, processes, curriculums, ideas. The world. And funnily enough, a 4 year degree in Comp. Sci. isn't the worst course to understand how design works.
